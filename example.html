Tips for monetizing autosurf traffic

You should take a slightly different approach when using autosurfs. Splash pages will still work, but with a much lower clickthrough rate than in manual exchanges. Here are some useful tips:
  • Earn banner impressions with every view. Since autosurfs can deliver massive amounts of hits, you can rake in those impressions and then get your banners shown all over the web! HitLink and 247AutoHits both have banner exchanges and allow autosurf traffic. Make a splash page promoting your favorite opportunity and place the banner codes in a visible location. Even if most visitors won't see your site, you will still earn banner views!
  • Get credits for promoting your referral links. Exchanges like SmileyTraffic and ClickVoyager give you credits or points for showing your referral URL. Using autosurfs is an easy way to earn credits without clicking!
  • Convert credits to banner impressions directly. You can do it in Twistrix, 247AutoHits, and several other TEs. Sites using VS-TrEx script like Yibbida also allow you to add banners or text ads and use autosurf credits to show them. You might be surprised, but my banners there actually get pretty good clickthrough rates! This is because they are displayed not only on the surfbar, but in the member area as well. As such, they're seen by members who log in to add sites, upgrade or assign credits.
  • Splash pages still work, and even though the CTR will be much lower, some people will still see your site (when launching and closing the surfbar, checking on it or watching for bonus links and so on). I promote this page in autosurfs and get signups from it all the time! The key is driving as much traffic as possible. Since you don't have to click, there's nothing stopping you from running 30 or more auto surfs at the same time.
